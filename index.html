Privacy Policy

WokyToky ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Business Owner (Manager) Accounts

  • Email address and password (encrypted)
  • Company name and contact information

Worker Accounts

  • Workers join using an invite code through anonymous authentication — no email or password is required
  • Name, phone number, and preferred language

Usage Data

  • Chat messages (original and translated text)
  • Voice recordings — when you use voice input, audio is recorded and converted to text (speech-to-text)
  • Attendance records (check-in/check-out times)
  • GPS location data (only during attendance check)
  • Photos and videos shared in chat
  • Push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To send push notifications for messages and announcements
  • To verify attendance using GPS location
  • To translate messages between languages
  • To convert voice input into text (speech recognition)
  • To manage employee attendance records

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database) with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled. Each company's data is completely isolated from other companies. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Requests to external processing services are routed through our secure server so that API credentials are never exposed in the app.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Database and authentication
  • Google Cloud Translation API — Message translation (processed via our secure server)
  • Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API — Voice-to-text conversion (processed via our secure server)
  • Expo Push Notifications — Push notification delivery
  • Firebase (FCM) — Android push notifications

When you use translation or voice input, the relevant message text or audio is sent to Google's services for processing through our secure server. This data is used only to return the translation or transcription result.

5. Location Data

GPS location is only collected at the moment of attendance check-in/check-out. Location data is used solely to verify that employees are within the designated work area. We do not track location continuously.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you request account deletion, your data — including chat messages, attendance records, and announcements — is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. Certain records may be retained where required by applicable law (for example, tax or payment records) for the period mandated by law. To request deletion, see our account deletion page or contact us using the details below.

7. Children's Privacy

WokyToky is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
